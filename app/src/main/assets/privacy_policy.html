🛡️ Privacy Policy for FitSathi

Last Updated: May 05, 2026

👋 1. Welcome to Your Fitness Journey

Welcome to FitSathi! We're committed to being a transparent and trustworthy partner in your health and fitness journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. Your privacy is not just a policy for us; it's a priority.

📊 2. The Information We Collect

To provide a personalized and effective fitness experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Your Profile Data: Name, email (for authentication), age, gender, height, weight, activity level, fitness goals, and preferred workout settings (location, duration, difficulty).
  • Your Fitness & Health Data: Daily step counts, calorie intake logs, water consumption, weight history, and workout progress/streaks.
  • Physical Activity: We use your device's sensors (via Android Activity Recognition) to track your steps in real-time.
  • Camera Access: Used only when you choose to scan product barcodes for nutritional information. Images are processed on-device and are not stored.
⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to power app features and improve your experience:

  • Goal Calculation: Using your age, weight, height, and activity level to calculate personalized calorie and hydration goals.
  • Progress Tracking: Visualizing your steps, nutrition, and weight trends over time.
  • Background Processing: We run a background service to ensure your steps are counted accurately even when the app is not actively in use.
  • Reminders: Setting notifications for meals and hydration based on your preferences.
🤝 4. Third-Party Partners & Services

We do not sell your personal information. To provide certain features, we share only necessary data with these trusted partners:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for secure account authentication, cloud database storage, and file hosting (profile pictures).
  • Nutritionix & Open Food Facts: Used to retrieve nutritional data when you search for food or scan a barcode. Only your search queries or barcode numbers are sent to these services.
  • Google ML Kit: Powers our on-device barcode scanner for fast and private product recognition.
🔐 5. Data Security & Retention

We implement robust technical and administrative security measures, including Firebase's secure infrastructure, to protect your data. Your data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services.

💪 6. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit Profile: Update your personal information anytime in the Profile section.
  • Global Reset: Use the "Reset Data" feature in Settings to instantly clear all local logs, cache, and sign out of your account.
  • Data Deletion: To completely erase your account and all associated cloud data, please contact us at the email below.
  • Permissions: You can revoke Camera or Activity Recognition permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will disable related features.
📜 7. Changes to This Policy

As FitSathi evolves with new features, we may update this policy. We will notify you of any significant changes via app notifications or within the app itself. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

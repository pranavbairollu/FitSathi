🚀 Welcome to Your FitSathi Guide!

🌟 Key Features
  • 👟 Step Counter: Tracks your daily steps automatically. Tap the progress circle beside the step count number to set or update your personal daily goal!
  • 🍽️ Calorie Tracker: Log your meals easily by searching our food database or scanning barcodes. The app calculates your recommended daily calories based on your profile, activity level, and fitness mission.
  • 💧 Water Tracker: Tap the water tracker card on the home screen to quickly log your intake. Stay hydrated and track your daily water goals.
  • 🏋️ Workout Feature: Get workout plans tailored to your fitness goal. Tap the "Goal" button at the top of the home screen to update your mission anytime!
  • 📈 Weight Tracker: Log your weight regularly and watch your progress over time through intuitive charts with motivational trend lines.
💡 Pro-Gamer Moves (Tips & Tricks)
  • 🔥 Build That Streak!: Log at least one food item per calendar day to maintain your streak and build healthy habits.
  • 🎉 Celebrate Your Wins! Fun animations appear when you achieve your step goals!
  • 💪 Long-Press to Delete: Mistakenly logged a food item? Long-press any entry to remove it instantly.
🤔 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
How is my streak calculated?

Your streak depends on consistency. Log at least one meal (breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack) per day. Missing a day will reset the streak, so stay consistent!

How do I change my fitness goal?

On the home screen, tap the "Goal" button next to your welcome message. Choose a new fitness mission, like Weight Loss or Muscle Gain. Your workout plans update automatically.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! You can log food, water, and track steps without internet. Your data is stored locally and syncs to your account once you're back online.

Why is my chart or graph empty?

If you haven't logged data for a specific metric yet (like weight or calories), the chart will appear empty. Start logging and watch it come to life!

How does Dark Mode work?

You can toggle Dark Mode manually in the Settings menu under the Appearance section. Use it for better accessibility and night-time use!

🛡️ Privacy Policy for FitSathi

Last Updated: October 07, 2025

👋 1. Welcome to Your Fitness Journey

Welcome to FitSathi! We're committed to being a trustworthy partner in your fitness journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app. Your privacy is a top priority.

📊 2. The Information We Collect

To give you a personalized experience, we collect a few types of information:

  • Your Profile Data: Name, email, age, gender, fitness goals, and profile picture.
  • Your Fitness & Health Data: Daily steps, calorie intake, water consumption, weight logs, workout history, streaks.
  • Camera Access: Only when using barcode scanner. Images processed on-device; not stored.
⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to power app features:

  • Manage your account.
  • Track fitness progress with charts and stats.
  • Personalize experience (workouts, goal celebrations).
  • Enable optional features like barcode scanning.
🤝 4. Who We Share Information With

We do not sell your info. Shared only with essential partners:

  • Firebase: Auth, Database, Storage for account and fitness data.
  • Google ML Kit: Powers on-device barcode scanner.
🔐 5. Keeping Your Information Secure

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures, including Firebase's security, to protect your data. No system is 100% impenetrable.

💪 6. Your Rights & Control Over Your Data

Your rights include:

  • Access & Correct: View and edit your profile anytime.
  • Deletion: Contact us to erase account and associated data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions like camera access; some features may stop working.
📜 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated; review periodically to stay informed.

🧸 8. A Note About Children's Privacy

Not intended for children under 13. If a child under 13 has provided information, contact us immediately for action.

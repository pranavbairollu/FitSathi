🚀 Welcome to Your FitSathi Guide!

Last Updated: May 05, 2026

🌟 Key Features
  • 👟 Step Counter: Tracks your daily steps automatically using your phone's activity sensors. Tap the progress circle beside the step count on the Home screen to set or update your personal daily goal!
  • 🍽️ Calorie Tracker: Log your meals by searching our database or scanning barcodes. The app calculates your daily calorie and macronutrient targets based on your profile, activity level, and fitness mission.
  • 💧 Water Tracker: Stay hydrated by logging your intake. You can quickly see your progress (e.g., "0/8 glasses") on the Home screen or tap the card for detailed logging.
  • 🏋️ Workout Plans: Get a customized daily workout based on your goals. Tap the "Goal" button at the top of the Home screen to update your mission (Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, etc.) anytime.
  • 📈 Weight Tracker: Log your weight to see your progress on a trend chart. Set a Target Weight to see a visual goal line and track how close you are to your objective.
💡 Pro-Tips & Control
  • 🔥 Maintain Your Streak: Your streak increases every calendar day you log at least one meal. Stay consistent to watch your number grow!
  • 🎉 Goal Celebrations: Achieving your daily step goal triggers a special celebration. Can you hit it every day?
  • 💪 Edit or Remove Entries: Tap any food entry in the Tracker to edit its serving size. Long-press an entry to remove it if you made a mistake.
  • 🔐 Global Reset: Found in Settings, this feature allows you to instantly sign out and wipe all local data from your device for a fresh start or privacy.
🤔 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
How is my streak calculated?

Your streak counts the consecutive days you've logged at least one food item. If you miss a day, the counter resets. We use UTC time for consistent global tracking.

How do I change my fitness mission?

On the Home screen, tap the "Goal" button in the top-right corner. You can change your primary goal, difficulty level, and workout preferences there.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! FitSathi supports offline logging. Your steps, water, and food entries are saved locally and will automatically sync with your cloud account once you have an internet connection.

Why is my chart empty?

Charts require data to display. Start logging your weight or meals, and the charts will populate automatically to show your trends over the last week or month.

How do I enable Dark Mode?

Go to Settings and toggle the Dark Mode switch. The app will instantly transition to a sleek dark theme, perfect for late-night logging.
